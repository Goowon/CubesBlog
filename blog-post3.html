How to Protect Yourself from Scammers in Animal Crossing New Horizons


Being a part of the Animal Crossing community is great! There are so many benefits to playing with other in Animal Crossing such as:

  • Getting people to water your flowers for extra bonuses!
  • Catalogue/Touch Trading with others to collect different furniture colors
  • Taking a departing villager off another person's island and onto yours
  • ...and so much more!

Although the community is great for helping each other out, there are some who abuse these tools and steal and wreck havoc on strangers' islands.

Do not be alarmed! As long as you're careful, any trade you do can be secured and you don't have to worry about being scammed!

If you are scammed while you are on an island or someone else is on your island, you need to be fast and hit the power button or sleep your switch. Both of these methods will instantly disconnect your switch from the internet and it will take you back to your respective islands as if nothing happened.

How it works is that whenever a visitor enters or leaves and island, the game saves the states of all people currently on that island. Using this method, you're not giving the game the chance to save what happened!

Knowing this piece of information, there's no need to shy away from interactions knowing that you have this tool to protect yourself!
